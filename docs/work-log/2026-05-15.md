The hard rules say "Keep daily diffs under ~400 lines unless the
roadmap item explicitly demands more; if larger, split into a
new sub-item in ROADMAP and only do the first sub-item today." —
splitting an item into sub-items is explicitly endorsed. R14a is
genuinely independent of R14b: the *procedure* (one-time PyPI
trusted-publisher setup, the tag-and-push flow, the manual
fallback, the verification checklist) is reviewable and useful
even before the automation that implements it lands. R14b stays
in *Up next* and the daily task will keep picking it up until
the PAT scope is fixed.
