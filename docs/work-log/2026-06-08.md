## Roadmap item
**R29. `apg audit stats <log>` summary subcommand.**

A one-screen plain-text summary of a JSONL audit log: total record count,
first/last timestamp, counts by verdict, top-N rules and top-N tools by hit
count, and the deny+review share of all decisions. Exit codes mirror
`apg-replay`: 0 ok, 2 missing file, 3 malformed log.

## Plan (smallest change satisfying the acceptance criteria)
- Add a pure `summarize_audit(records, *, source, top_n)` helper to
  `audit.py` that turns an iterable of `AuditRecord` into a stable list of
  output lines (logic separated from I/O, mirroring `cli._explain`/`_lint`).

## Decisions / notes
- The `Verdict` enum has four members (`allow`/`deny`/`review`/`redact`), so the
  summary shows all four; the **deny+review share** line counts only deny+review
  exactly as the acceptance criteria specify.
- Timestamps use `min`/`max` over the records (ISO-8601 sorts chronologically),
  robust to any out-of-order lines.
